About College :

SSM Institute of Engineering and Technology was established in 2011. It is a private institute located in Dindigul, Tamil Nadu. SSM Institute of Engineering and Technology is approved by AICTE. SSM Institute of Engineering and Technology offers a variety of courses at UG and PG levels. These 2 courses are offered in specialisations such as Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electronics Engineering and Cyber Security and others. B.E. / B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech are some of the courses offered by SSM Institute of Engineering and Technology. All courses at the institute are offered in Full Time mode. SSM Institute of Engineering and Technology offers admission to its 546 seats. The average fees for B.E. / B.Tech and M.E./M.Tech ranges from INR 50,000 to 2,00,000. The average median salary offered as per the official website of SSM Institute of Engineering and Technology is INR 2,35,333.
